Γ = ⟨a, b⟩ is a 2-generator Kleinian group. BFS walks the Cayley graph of the free group F₂, appending one of {a, A=a⁻¹, b, B=b⁻¹} per step and forbidding the inverse of the previous letter so paths are reduced words. For each visited word w we draw its isometric circle I(w) = {z : |w′(z)| = 1} in faint blue — every descendant of w lies inside I(w), so we prune a branch when I(w) shrinks below the pixel threshold ε. Leaf words (depth = max) plot their fixed points in yellow, which accumulate on the limit set Λ(Γ).
